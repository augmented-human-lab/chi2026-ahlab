15 Years

Extending Human Potential

In the last fifteen years, Augmented Human Lab has pursued one vision: a paradigm shift in the design of user interface systems, where technology becomes a seamless extension of the human body, mind, and identity rather than a separate tool.

Through carefully designed Assistive Augmentations, we aim to empower people constrained by impairments to live more independently while extending human perceptual, cognitive, and creative capabilities beyond the ordinary.
